My experience

I studied Computer Science(BAMN-DTEK) at the University of Bergen.

During my studies, I volunteered twice at the Java-conference JavaZone.

After ending my studies, I worked with a small startup (Sponsor1) with the goal of enabling wealth-redistribution targeted at small/amateur sports-organizations and other volunteer-based organizations. I got to work with simple dev-ops, version-control, PHP / CodeIgniter, in order to make a login/control panel for users of this system.

In 2017, I got hired by Develo 1881, a rapidly growing Web Development/Design Agency. My accomplishments here includes Employee of the Year 2018, and lead developer on multiple UmbracoCMS-projects.
